📜 How to Play

Goal

Endless waves march on your gate along one, two, or three attack roads (pick your battlefield). Leaked enemies cost lives — bosses cost 10. Survive as long as you can.

🏰 Towers

  • 11 types, 5 upgrade levels each. Armored foes shrug off arrows — melt them with magic (❄🔥⚡☠).
  • Barricades are built ON the road — enemies must batter them down before they can pass. The Arbalest is a slow, monstrous sniper that prioritizes flyers.
  • Double-click (or double-tap) to build: first click aims the ghost, second click builds it.
  • ↩ undoes your last build or upgrade at full refund. ⏫ Max on a tower — or the ⏫ All button in each panel — buys upgrades in bulk. ⏩ blitzes the next 10 waves at hyper speed.
  • Maxed towers and troops can be promoted: ⟡ Silver, then ★ Gold — big permanent power. Heroes can rarely ✨ Ascend when trained; legends and the ascended can even go 🔥 DIVINE.
  • ☄️ Calamities are ultra-rare wandering horrors — slay one for an artifact trophy. Elites sometimes drop hero gear. The 🗺 Frontier (Relics panel) buys a bigger battlefield.
  • Gold Mint = passive income; Holy Beacon buffs nearby towers. Click a built tower to upgrade or sell.

⚔️ Army

  • 12 troop types unlock over the waves. Set each type's keep-count (− / +) and fallen troops are auto-resummoned with your gold — the war continues while you work.
  • On multi-road maps the army automatically reinforces the most threatened road. R sets rally points.

🦸 Heroes — up to six!

  • Sir Aldric fights from wave 1. New champions unlock as you push deeper — recruit them with gold.
  • Train heroes to raise their power; at Lv 3 they learn a signature skill (auto-cast), at Lv 8 a passive.
  • Click a hero (or H) then click the map to reposition them.

💎 Relics

Permanent, tiered upgrades: sharper troops, stronger towers, bigger army, richer economy, tougher walls.

⚡ Spells

  • Three free spells on the buttons over the battlefield, recharging on their own: Firestorm (click-target burn), Sanctified Ground (click-target heal zone), and RAGNAROK — a long-cooldown cataclysm that devastates every enemy, resummons your whole army free, and empowers it. Save it for the clutch.

✨ Events, Legendaries & the Vault

  • Watch for the Gilded Boar — kill it for gold, free upgrades, or spell resets.
  • Shadow Wardens rarely appear dragging a captive: slay one and a Legendary Hero (Aurelia, Karrgoth, or Morrigan) joins you — recorded in your Vault forever, free to summon in every future run.
  • The ⭐ Peak button on a battlefield restarts at wave 1 with your best-ever heroes, relics, troop levels and a rebuild budget — grind once, keep it always.

✈ Flying enemies

After the first boss, the sky fills: Fell Bats, Gargoyles, Harpies, Storm Wyverns. Only towers badged ⛰＋✈ and ranged troops/heroes can hit them — melee can't touch the sky, and flyers sail right over your blockers. Keep anti-air on every road and brace for AIR RAID waves.

🎡 Wheel of Fortune

Every new battle opens with one free spin — gold, troop levels, hero levels, relic tiers… and one tiny golden JACKPOT slice: +1000g and +1 to everything.

😎 Multitask mode

AUTO waves on → set army keep-counts → build Gold Mints → 3× speed → collapse the bottom panel (▼) and get to work. Check in to spend gold. Progress autosaves every wave, per battlefield.
